Pizza

Ingredients

  • Take out/delivery menu for pizzeria
  • Phone
  • Credit Card

Steps

  1. Choose a pizza you want to order per the menu
  2. Have your address and credit card information easily accessible
  3. Call the phone number on the menu and order your pizza
  4. If requesting "delivery", wait for pizza to arrive. If requesting 'take out', drive/walk/bike to the pizzeria to pick up your order
